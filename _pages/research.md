<div class="cv-body">
<p>My work sits across political ecology, critical agrarian studies, and critical political economy. Below are the broad lines of enquiry I pursue.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="cv-row">
<div class="cv-label">Agrarian change<br>& fire</div>
<div class="cv-body">
<p><em>How do transformations in land, labour, and rural livelihoods shape fire regimes — and what does fire reveal about dispossession, commons, and forest dependence?</em></p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="cv-row">
<div class="cv-label">State-making</div>
<div class="cv-body">
<p><em>How are disaster framings, blame narratives, and fire suppression policies used to extend state authority — and to criminalise traditional fire use?</em></p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="cv-row">
<div class="cv-label">Relational<br>approaches to fire</div>
<div class="cv-body">
<p><em>How do communities live with fire rather than simply control it — and what do relational and more-than-human frameworks offer for understanding fire governance?</em></p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="cv-row">
<div class="cv-label">Plantation economies<br>& wildfire risk</div>
<div class="cv-body">
<p><em>How does plantation expansion concentrate wildfire risk and reconfigure the political and ecological conditions under which fires spread?</em></p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="cv-row">
<div class="cv-label">Political economy<br>of insurance</div>
<div class="cv-body">
<p><em>How does private insurance reshape understandings of loss, responsibility, and climate adaptation — and how does its uneven reach reproduce inequalities of race, class, and geography?</em></p>
</div>
</div>
